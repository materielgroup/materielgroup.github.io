Secure Your Supply Chain

The Vulnerability of Critical Mineral Acquisition

The Defense Industrial Base faces significant risks in sourcing essential minerals, threatening manufacturing capabilities.

Spot Market Volatility

Exposure to unpredictable price swings and inconsistent supply availability.

Unreliable Offtake Agreements

Often short-term and subject to disruptive price fluctuations.

Costly Stockpiles

Self-run and government stockpiles are expensive to maintain with uncertain access.

Current and impending changes to US government contracting structure enforce the need to internalize supply chain risk management. Reliance on these traditional methods is no longer sustainable.
